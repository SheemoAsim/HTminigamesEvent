Calculation Approach

Each stage contributes equally. Final = Average of all stages (with difficulty multipliers).

  • Stage 1: (Game1 Score(0-400pts)% + Game2 Least Time(0-20min)% + Game3 Right answer(0/6-6/6)%) / 3 × 1.0
  • Stage 2: (Game1 Score(0-150pts)% + Game2 Least Time(0-20min)% + Game3 Score(0-150pts)%) / 3 × 1.25
  • Stage 3: (Game3 Score(0-150pts)% + Game3 Score(0-150pts)% + Game3 Score(0-150pts)%) / 3 × 1.5

Example: Stage1=80%, Stage2=90%, Stage3=85% → Final = (80+90+85)/3 = 85%
